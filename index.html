Alexander Shulgin

  • Alexander Shulgin was an chemist at the University of California - Berkley. He synthesized MDMA and contributed to its growth as a party drug from 1965 to the present.
  • Shulgin also wrote 2 chemical cookbooks (PIHKAL and TIHKAL) that included recipes for various compounds that he had discovered. The two books also included an autobiography of his life with his wife Ann.
  • Shulgin was pivotal to the introduction of MDMA for psychotheraphy in the 1970s.
